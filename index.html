// The Real Problem

You Don't Have a
Strategy Problem.

Every trading forum, YouTube channel and paid course will sell you another strategy. Another indicator. Another setup. But walk into any prop firm, speak to any consistently profitable trader, and they'll tell you the same thing — discipline, routine and psychology kill more accounts than bad strategies ever will.

The hardest part of trading isn't finding an edge. It's following your rules when your emotions are screaming at you not to. It's maintaining your pre-market routine when you're tired. It's having a written plan that keeps you accountable to yourself when nobody else is watching.

You trade without a written plan

Most traders carry their rules in their head. That works fine — until a losing streak, a big win, or a volatile open. Then the rules disappear.

Your plan exists but you don't follow it

A plan in a Word document you haven't opened in three months isn't a plan. It's a good intention.

You know what to do. You just don't do it.

This is the hardest truth in trading. The gap between knowing and doing costs traders more than any bad strategy ever could.

// The Solution

Build Your Plan.
Follow Your Plan.
Become Consistent.

TradingPlan is a native Apple app — built for iPhone, iPad and Mac — that guides you through building a complete, professional trading plan and then puts it to work every single trading day.

Your Trading Why

Consistency starts with purpose. TradingPlan begins where every great trading journey does — understanding why you trade and what you're building toward. When the hard days come, this is what keeps you disciplined.

Pre-Market Checklist

The foundation of daily consistency. Build your own pre-market routine and check it off every morning before you place a single trade. Never start a session unprepared again.

Trading Philosophy Builder

What kind of trader are you? What do you value? What's your edge? TradingPlan walks you through defining your identity as a trader — so your decisions have a foundation.

Psychology Framework

Fear, greed, revenge trading, overtrading — TradingPlan helps you identify your personal psychological triggers and build specific rules to address them before they cost you money.

Rules of Engagement

Your strategy rules, analysis criteria and trade execution rules — all structured, all in one place, all one tap away when you need to check yourself.

Risk & Money Management

The section most traders skip. TradingPlan makes risk management a non-negotiable part of your plan — with clear rules for position sizing, drawdown limits and daily loss caps.

01

Work through guided sections to create your complete trading plan. Each section prompts the right questions. You provide the answers. The result is a professional, personalised plan that reflects how you actually trade.

02
