The Complete System

Every layer of your trading process.

From writing your strategy to checking off every rule before a trade — TradingPlan covers the full process.

Strategy Builder

Build a complete strategy with rules across six sections — directional bias, analysis, entry, stop loss, target, and trade management. Long and short rules kept separate. Everything in one place.

Strategy Flow

When it's time to trade, launch the flow. Step through every rule. Check it off. The app tracks what passed and what didn't. Stop entering trades on gut feel.
