Upgrading to Pro

How to Subscribe

  1. Go to Profile in the app
  2. Tap Upgrade to Pro (or tap any feature marked with a Pro badge)
  3. Review the subscription options and pricing
  4. Confirm your purchase with Face ID, Touch ID, or Apple ID password

Your Pro features activate immediately after the purchase is confirmed.

Subscription Options

TradingPlan Pro is available as a monthly or annual subscription. Pricing is displayed in your local currency on the upgrade screen. Annual plans offer a significant saving over monthly billing.

All payments are handled by Apple. Your payment method on file with your Apple ID is charged.

What Happens After You Subscribe

  • Pro features unlock immediately — no restart required
  • Your strategy limit increases to unlimited
  • Your routine limit increases to unlimited
  • All eight advanced risk sections become available
  • The Pro upgrade widget disappears from your Dashboard

Restore a Purchase

If you've previously subscribed and Pro features aren't active — for example, after reinstalling the app or signing in on a new device — you can restore your subscription:

  1. Go to Profile
  2. Tap Upgrade to Pro
  3. On the upgrade screen, tap Restore Purchase

Your subscription will be verified with Apple and your Pro access will be restored. You won't be charged again.

Note: Restore Purchase only works if you're signed in with the same Apple ID that made the original purchase.

Free Trial

If a free trial is available, it will be displayed on the upgrade screen before you confirm. Free trials are managed entirely by Apple — you won't be charged until the trial period ends, and you can cancel at any time before it ends.

Sharing with Family (Family Sharing)

TradingPlan Pro does not currently support Family Sharing. Each person who wants Pro access needs their own subscription.

Problems After Purchase?

See Subscription Not Activating if your Pro features haven't unlocked after subscribing.
