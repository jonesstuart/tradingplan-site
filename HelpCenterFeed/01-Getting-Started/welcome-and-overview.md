# Welcome to TradingPlan

**Category:** Getting Started  
**Helpful for:** New users, users who aren't sure what the app does

---

## What is TradingPlan?

TradingPlan is a personal trading discipline app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. It helps you build, document, and execute your trading plan — so you trade by your rules, not by your emotions.

Most traders lose not because they lack knowledge, but because they lack structure. TradingPlan fixes that by giving you a framework to:

- Define who you are as a trader (philosophy, mindset, why you trade)
- Build strategies with precise, step-by-step rules
- Check yourself against your rules before entering any trade
- Manage your risk with defined limits and protocols
- Run structured routines before, during, and after your trading sessions
- Keep your head straight with mindset prompts and motivational content

---

## The Core Idea

Before you enter a trade, you should be able to answer: **"Does this trade meet my rules?"**

TradingPlan makes that question easy to answer by putting your entire plan in your pocket. Everything you've decided about how you trade — your edge, your rules, your risk limits — lives in one place. Then, when a trade opportunity appears, you run your strategy flow and let your rules make the call. Not your gut. Not market noise. Your plan.

## What's in the App

### Dashboard
Your home screen. Shows a customisable set of widgets: your strategies, active routines, a motivational quote, your philosophy summary, mindset notes, and more. You can choose which widgets appear and reorder them to suit how you work.

### Trade Plan
The heart of the app. This is where you build your trading identity:
- **Philosophy** — your beliefs about markets, your analytical approach, your trading values and style
- **Risk Management** — how you size positions, your drawdown limits, and (on Pro) advanced risk rules
- **Mindset** — personal notes on the mental game
- **Your Why** — your deeper motivation for trading
- **Routines** — structured checklists for your trading sessions
- **Business Notes** — a running log of key observations and decisions

### Strategies
Where your trading strategies live. Each strategy has rules for entry, stop-loss, targets, directional bias, active management, and analysis. You can create your own from scratch or study the example strategies included in the app.

### Plan Builder
An interactive flow that walks you through the whole plan-building process step by step — useful when you're first setting up or reviewing and updating your plan.

---

## This App Is Not

- A trading platform — it doesn't connect to brokers or execute trades
- A signal service — it doesn't tell you when to trade
- A journalling app — it doesn't log trade history or P&L
- Financial advice — everything in the app is for planning and discipline purposes
