# Managing Your Subscription

**Category:** Account & Subscription  
**Helpful for:** Users who want to cancel, change plan, or understand billing

---

## Where Subscriptions Are Managed

TradingPlan Pro subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple. TradingPlan doesn't handle billing directly — Apple processes all payments and manages subscription terms.

To manage your subscription, you need to go through Apple's subscription management — not the TradingPlan app itself.

---

## How to Cancel or Change Your Plan

### On iPhone or iPad
1. Open the **Settings** app
2. Tap your name at the top (Apple ID)
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find **TradingPlan** in the list
5. Tap it to see options: cancel, change between monthly and annual, or view renewal date

### On Mac
1. Open the **App Store**
2. Click your name / account icon at the bottom of the sidebar
3. Click **Manage Subscriptions**
4. Find **TradingPlan** and click to manage

### Via Apple's Website
Go to [appleid.apple.com](https://appleid.apple.com), sign in, and go to **Subscriptions**.

---

## What Happens When You Cancel

- Pro features remain active until the end of the current billing period
- After the billing period ends, the app reverts to the free plan
- Your data is not deleted — all your strategies, routines, and plan content remain intact
- If you have more than one strategy, you'll see a prompt to choose which one to keep on the free plan (the others won't be deleted, but you'll need to choose which is active)
- The same applies to routines if you have more than one

> Your content is safe. Cancelling Pro just changes which features are unlocked — it never deletes your data.

---

## Resubscribing

If you cancel and later want Pro again:
1. Go to **Profile → Upgrade to Pro**
2. Subscribe as normal

Your previous data (strategies, routines, plan) will still be there from when you last used the app.

---

## Billing Questions

For questions about a specific charge, billing date, refund, or payment method, contact Apple directly:
- **Apple Support:** [support.apple.com](https://support.apple.com)
- **Request a refund:** [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)

TradingPlan cannot process refunds — all billing is handled by Apple.

---

## Checking Your Renewal Date

Your next renewal date is visible in Apple's Subscription management screen (Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → TradingPlan on iPhone/iPad).
