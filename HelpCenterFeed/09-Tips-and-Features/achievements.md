# Achievements

**Category:** Tips & Features  
**Helpful for:** Users who want to know about the achievement system, users who saw an achievement notification

---

## What Are Achievements?

Achievements are milestones that unlock as you build and use your trading plan. They're a way of recognising the work you're putting in — and they mark real progress: creating your first strategy, completing your first strategy flow, building a complete plan, developing a consistent routine habit.

When you unlock an achievement, a toast notification appears briefly at the bottom of the screen with the achievement name and icon.

---

## Why Achievements?

Building a complete trading plan takes effort. Most traders know they should do it but never get all the pieces in place. Achievements make the progress visible and give you a sense of momentum as you build out your plan section by section.

They're not just cosmetic — each achievement represents a genuine milestone in the plan-building process.

---

## How to See Your Achievements

Go to **Profile**. Your unlocked achievements are displayed in the profile view.

---

## Example Achievements

Achievements cover milestones like:

- **First Strategy** — Create your first trading strategy
- **Plan Builder** — Complete a run through the Plan Builder
- **First Flow** — Run your first strategy flow
- **Consistent Trader** — Complete multiple strategy flows
- **Routine Habit** — Complete a set number of routine flows
- **Complete Plan** — Have every section of your trade plan filled in
- **Elite Planner** — Achieve the highest planning milestone — a fully built, actively used trading system

The full list is discoverable in the Profile section — some achievements have specific criteria you'll need to figure out.

---

## Do Achievements Affect Anything?

Achievements are recognition only — they don't unlock features or change how the app works. They're a measure of progress, not a gate.

---

## What Triggers the Review Prompt?

TradingPlan may occasionally ask you to rate the app. These prompts are tied to specific positive milestones:
- After completing your first strategy flow
- After completing several routine flows (habit formed)
- After unlocking the Elite Planner achievement (complete system built)

These are the moments where you've genuinely experienced the value of the app — making it the most natural time to ask. You'll never be asked more than once per milestone, and you can always dismiss the prompt.
